What changed in this refinement. Emoji glyphs replaced with a stroke-based icon set (2dp, rounded caps, 24px grid — the design-system spec); tighter type scale and letter-spacing; softer, layered depth (real shadows in light, borders in dark); a proper two-line split-flap roll on the board; a gradient route line and dashed estimated marker on the map; refined chips, sheet, and tab bar with an active-state indicator. Same flows as docs/ux-redesign.md. Try: tap the live board → Journey surface; type 129, 4392, or ndls to bpl in Search; open a train and tap the sun for Sunlight mode; drag the sheet grabber.
