What you're looking at. This is a clickable HTML prototype of docs/ux-redesign.md — not the Compose build. It renders the redesign's real design tokens (Paper/Board surfaces, brand-vs-signal colour, the dashed estimate style, mono tabular numerals) and the new IA. Try: tap the live board on Home → the merged Journey surface (map + spine + coach guide + PNR pass); type 129, 4392, or ndls to bpl in Search to watch it classify; open a train and tap ☀ to preview Sunlight mode; drag the sheet grabber to resize the map. The board flap-rolls when the delay changes (watch the live card a few seconds after landing). Reduced-motion is honoured.
